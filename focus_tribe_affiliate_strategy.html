Target Audience & Client Avatar

Primary Avatar — "The Ambitious Drifter"

  • Age 23–38, remote worker or solopreneur
  • Has goals (business, fitness, creative project, skill)
  • Keeps starting and stopping — knows what to do, can't stay consistent
  • Already consumes self-improvement content
  • Responds to social commitment and real consequences
  • Values structure over motivation

Secondary Avatar — "The Solopreneur"

  • Freelancer, creator, or online business owner
  • No boss or team = no external accountability
  • Has a specific income or business goal
  • Willing to invest in accountability tools
  • Needs structure because self-discipline alone isn't enough
  • Values community of like-minded doers

Why They Buy

  • $27/month is an easy yes if they feel genuine accountability
  • 7-day free trial removes all risk
  • They've spent more on courses they never finished
  • The community = the product (not information)
  • Public commitment creates real social pressure
  • Real consequences make it stick

Core Pain Points to Speak To

Smaller creators often have higher engagement and better conversion. Avoid mega-influencers (1M+) — they rarely do affiliate-only deals.

Niche (must match 2–3)

  • Productivity & habit building
  • Personal development / self-improvement
  • Goal setting, accountability, journaling
  • Morning routines / daily rituals
  • Mindfulness, intentional living
  • Solopreneurship / freelance life
  • Study with me / coworking / deep work

Engagement Rate (Minimum)

3%+ on Instagram / TikTok

5%+ like-to-view on YouTube

Active comment sections matter more than raw follower count. Look for real conversations, not just emojis.

Content Style ✓

  • Authentic, personal, "real life"
  • "With me" formats (study/work/habit vlogs)
  • Educational or challenge-based
  • Already promotes tools or communities
  • English-speaking audience (primarily)

Disqualifiers ✗

  • Audience primarily teenagers
  • Pure motivational quotes, no real engagement
  • Fake follower % above 30%
  • Non-English content
  • Pure entertainment / gaming / fashion
  • Content feels inauthentic or transactional

Outreach Tips

  • Reference a specific video or post
  • Offer a free membership first
  • Lead with recurring income angle
  • Keep it personal, not templated
  • Follow up once if no reply after 7 days
